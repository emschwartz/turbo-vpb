TurboVPB

Turbocharge OpenVPB

TurboVPB is a free browser extension that speeds up EveryAction's Open Virtual Phone Bank (OpenVPB).

It sends each contact's details to your phone so you can call or text with the push of a button.
Make more calls instead of wasting time typing in phone numbers by hand!

Install Firefox Extension Beta Install Chrome Extension Beta

Getting Started

Once you have the extension installed:

  1. Login to an OpenVPB phone bank
  2. Scan the QR code that appears on the OpenVPB page
  3. Now, the TurboVPB page on your phone will have a button to call the phone number when each contact loads on OpenVPB

Sending Text Messages

TurboVPB can also be used to easily send text messages, for example if you cannot reach a contact or to send more information after a call.

  1. Open the exension options (if you have TurboVPB installed, click here).
  2. Fill in the Text Message Template(s) with your message content
  3. Now, the TurboVPB page on your phone will also have buttons to send the configured text messages. Also, TurboVPB will replace [Their Name] in the message with each contact's first name.
  4. You can also configure TurboVPB to record the Texted result and automatically load the next contact when you send a message to a contact you couldn't reach.

How it works

TurboVPB uses PeerJS, based on WebRTC, to connect the browser extension and the website opened on your phone. Contact details are sent directly from your browser to your phone. They are never sent to TurboVPB.

TurboVPB turns contact details into tel: and sms: links. When you click on the buttons on your phone, your phone will automatically open your phone or messaging app to call or send the text.

Troubleshooting

If you run into any issues, try closing the OpenVPB tab, opening a new one, and re-scanning the QR code.

If that doesn't work, please get in touch: help [at] turbovpb [dot] com.
