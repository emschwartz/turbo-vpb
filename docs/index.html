TurboVPB

Turbocharge OpenVPB Phonebanking

TurboVPB is a Firefox Extension that speeds up OpenVPB phonebanking. It automatically sends contact details to your phone so you can make more calls instead of wasting time typing in phone numbers.

Install Firefox Extension beta

Getting Started

Once you have the extension installed:

  1. Login to an OpenVPB phone bank
  2. Click TurboVPB icon in the toolbar to open the settings
  3. Scan the QR code on your phone to open the TurboVPB link
  4. Now, when you load each contact, the TurboVPB page on your phone will have links to quickly call each contact and send follow-up text messages

How it works

TurboVPB uses PeerJS, based on WebRTC, to connect the browser extension and the website opened on your phone. Contact details are sent directly from your browser to your phone. They are never sent to TurboVPB.

TurboVPB turns contact details into tel: and sms: links that are automatically handled by the phone and messaging apps on your phone.
