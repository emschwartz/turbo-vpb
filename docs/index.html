TurboVPB

Turbocharge OpenVPB

TurboVPB is a browser extension that speeds up EveryAction's OpenVPB. It automatically sends contact details to your phone so you can make more calls instead of wasting time typing in phone numbers.

Install Firefox Extension Beta

Getting Started

Once you have the extension installed:

  1. Login to an OpenVPB phone bank
  2. Scan the QR code that appears on the OpenVPB page
  3. Now, the TurboVPB page on your phone will have a link you can click to call when each contact loads on OpenVPB
  4. If you configure the follow-up text messages in TurboVPB's settings, there will be links to send each with your name and the contact's name automatically filled in.

How it works

TurboVPB uses PeerJS, based on WebRTC, to connect the browser extension and the website opened on your phone. Contact details are sent directly from your browser to your phone. They are never sent to TurboVPB.

TurboVPB turns contact details into tel: and sms: links. When you click on the buttons on your phone, your phone will automatically open your phone or messaging app to call or send the text.
