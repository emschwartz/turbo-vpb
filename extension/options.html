When you connect to TurboVPB on your phone, you will be able to call and/or text contacts with the push of a button.

To send text messages (optional), enter the content to send in the First Message and/or Second Message fields below. A button to send each text message will appear on your phone.


*Note: If this option is selected, after sending the message, TurboVPB will click the "I Couldn't Reach ___" button on OpenVPB, select the "Texted" option (if it is available), and click "Save & Next Call" to load the next contact.

Options are saved automatically.
